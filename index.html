Executive Order Establishing Task Force for Identifying and Reducing Fraud in Federal Benefit Programs

Original Post

This executive order establishes a task force to review and address potential fraudulent activities within the federal benefits system administered by states. The order acknowledges that American taxpayers fund various benefits programs providing housing assistance, food assistance, medical care, cash assistance, and other support services. The task force will focus on identifying fraud, waste, and improper payments within these federally funded programs that are administered at the state level. The order directs relevant federal agencies to coordinate efforts to detect and reduce fraudulent claims, improper enrollments, and misuse of federal benefits across multiple assistance programs.
